Our Profile

Vkellogg’s was founded in 1906 by culinary enthusiasts Will Keith Kellogg in Battle Creek, Michigan, United States. Initially starting as a small deli focusing on organic breakfast items, the company quickly gained popularity for its unique and nutritious offerings. In 1978, Vkellogg’s expanded its product line to include a variety of healthy snacks, catering to the growing demand for convenient yet nutritious food options. By 2004, Vkellogg’s products were available in major supermarkets across the United States, and by this year the company launched its online store to reach a global audience. Today, Vkellogg’s is a leading brand in the breakfast and snack food industry, known for its commitment to quality, health, and sustainability.

Vision

Vkellogg’s motivate us to be the world's leading provider of nutritious and delicious breakfast and snack options, fostering a healthier lifestyle and a sustainable future for all.

Mission

Vkellogg’s is dedicated to create high-quality, nutritious, and fascinating breakfast and snacks that inspire healthier living. We are committed to support local communities, ensuring that every product we offer not only tastes great but also contributes positively to the world. Our mission also to nourish people’s lives through exceptional food, ethical practices, and a passion for excellence.
