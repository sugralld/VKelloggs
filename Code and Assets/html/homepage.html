A Corn's Tale Breakfast

A bowl of appetizing Vkellogg’s Corn Flakes and creamy yoghurt studded with colourful berries.

Ingredients:

  • 20g blackberries
  • 20g strawberries
  • 2 tbsp pomegranate seeds
  • 1/2 passion fruit
  • 150g yoghurt
  • 30g VKellogg's corn flakes

How to make:

  1. Pour yoghurt to a bowl
  2. Add Vkellogg’s Corn Flakes
  3. Top with blackberrries, strawberries, and passionfruit
  4. Sprinkle on pomegranate seeds and serve
