A Corn's Tale Breakfast

A bowl of appetizing Vkellogg’s Corn Flakes and creamy yoghurt studded with colourful berries.

Ingredients:

  • 20g blackberries
  • 20g strawberries
  • 2 tbsp pomegranate seeds
  • 1/2 passion fruit
  • 150g yoghurt
  • 30g VKellogg's corn flakes

How to make:

  1. Pour yoghurt to a bowl
  2. Add Vkellogg’s Corn Flakes
  3. Top with blackberrries, strawberries, and passionfruit
  4. Sprinkle on pomegranate seeds and serve

Slice of Banana and Berries

Start your day by enjoying slice of banana and berries in a bowl of Vkellogg’s Bran Flakes combined with yogurt.

Ingredients:

  • 20g blackberries
  • 30g VKellogg's Bran Flakes
  • 150g yoghurt
  • 1 small banana
  • 20g blueberries

How to make:

  1. Pour yoghurt into a clean bowl.
  2. Incorporate Bran Flakes to add a creamy crunch.
  3. Put a hand of blueberries for a touch of sweetness.
  4. Arrange tidy banana slices on top.
  5. Present your bowl of yoghurt, Bran Flakes, blueberries, and banana. Savor every bite!

Garnola Bars

Crafted from the finest ingredients, these bars are made to be your perfect companion for any time of the day.

Ingredients:

  • 7 tbsp golden syrup
  • 50g packed brown sugar
  • 50g butter
  • 150g Vkellogg's Bran Flakes
  • 2tsp grated orange peel
  • 70g chopped mixed dried fruit (such as: apricots, dates, apples)

How to make:

  1. Start off by blending golden syrup, brown sugar, and butter in a sizable saucepan.
  2. Keep stirring over medium heat until the mixture starts bubbling and the sugar is fully dissolved.
  3. Next, add cereal, orange rind, and dried fruit.
  4. Transfer the mixture into a greased baking pan of roughly 23 cm square.
  5. Allow it to cool down at room temperature before carving it into delightful bars.
  6. Keep them stored in an airtight container and enjoy whenever you want.

Walnuts Cherries Bar

A bowl of delicious Vkellogg's Marshmallow Krispies and walnuts combined to a ball covered with chocolate.

Ingredients:

  • 55g Vkellogg's Marshmallow Krispies
  • 50g dried cherries
  • 50g walnuts
  • 100g chocolate
  • 40g butter or margarine
  • 120g marshmallows

How to make:

  1. In a large bowl, combine Vkellogg's Marshmallow Krispies, cherries, and nuts.
  2. Simultaneously, in a saucepan over low heat, melt the butter. Continue to stir until the mixture is smooth and then remove from heat.
  3. Pour this smoothly blended chocolate mixture into the large bowl and mix thoroughly to ensure the cereal is well coated.
  4. Transfer this mix into a lined 8 x 8 x 2-inch tray, pressing evenly to ensure a flat surface.
  5. Place the tray in the fridge to chill for about 30 minutes, allowing the mixture to set.
  6. After chilling, remove from the fridge and cut into squares of your preferred size.
